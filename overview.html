Overview

Orion V2.14 is a technical fest organized by IEEE student branch ECA under the coordination of IEEE Delhi Section that will be hosted at Govt. Engineering College, Ajmer from 11th April to 13th April 2014. Orion V2.14 is a collection of events which would somehow not only nourish technical knowledge and skills of the students but would also create an atmosphere which would give students a better platform to enhance their leadership and communication skills.
