Robo Soccer


Problem Statement
May be you are a big fan of Soccer but can you make your robot also feel like that, Let’s check it out…
Rules and Regulations
1. Machine should be controlled by a wired mechanism.
2. Machine must not be made from Lego parts or any readymade kit. If such parts are seen on the bot, team will be disqualified.
3. One member of team would be allowed to handle the bot at the time of competition.
4. Ball pick up mechanism is strictly prohibited.
5. Judging Criteria: Team which earns the maximum points during the game will be winner.(point rules will be displayed on the spot)
Task:
Participants have to make a simple manual robot to play soccer game, means to simply push the ball from one place to another.
