Description
The team work is very important part of team for winning. It is very important to have understanding smong them. If you think you have good coordination and understanding between you, so this competition is for you. So, boost up your adrenaline and brain power because we gonna suck them.
Certifiacte Policy
1. Winner will be provided certificate of merit.
2. 1st & 2nd runner ups will be awarded with certificate of participation.
Time Duration
Time duration will be of 1 hr and 30 mins.
Rules and Regulations
1.Each team should have atleast 1 laptop.
2.IDE named Dev-C++ must be oreinstalled on laptops.
3.You mst bring your own statinaries. Other than blank papers nothing will be provided to you.
Event Description
1. Event is single round.
2. It will be knockout contest.
3. Participants will be in a team of 3 members.
Gameplay
1. Every member of each team will be given a different problem statement which should be completed and submitted on it.
2. A team which does not complete their task they will be disqualify.
3. Intially, team members A, B, C will start working on the terminals having problems P1, P2, P3 respectively.
4. After 30 minutes, the members will cyclically shift their positions and continue the work of their team members on the problems of that position.
5. Hence every team member will get 30 minutes to work on each problem.
Note
1. Be prepared to face no. of twists that will be introduced at any time.
2. Some gameplay criteria will be disclosed at venue.
