Exhibition

Scientists study the world as it is,
engineers create the world that never has been


Problem Statement
Exhibitions at techfest have always been helpful in bridging the gap between imagination and technology. They are bolstering in spreading creativity and a sense of innovation among the nascent techies. We have been privileged in witnessing what the world has achieved and in acknowledging a whole new perspective of future through our exhibition, which revaluates the aspects of all technical streams.
THEMES:
• Ecoholix (models bridging environment and technology)
• Robotronix (Autonomous and manual robots)
• Architectural models
• Machine, Engines and Motors
• Pure Science
• Disaster Management
• Tech-Décor (technology in day to day life)
Rules and Regulations
The interested candidates, from all the branches have to submit an abstract describing the idea related to their theme. Selected entries will be selected for exhibition.
• The exhibit/project may include
(a) Working model to explain a concept, principle or a process.
(b) An indigenous design of a machine/device.
(c) An innovative/inexpensive design or technique.
(d) Application of basic principles of Science/Technology.
(e) Scheme/design of a device or machine to reduce production cost.
• One team can present only one model.
• A team can have 1 to 2 team members.
• The interested candidates, from all the branches, can contact the undersigned with their respective models.
Certification & Prizes
A certification of excellence will be given to top 3 teams,
First Prize: Rs.3000/-
Second Prize: Rs.1500/-
Third Prize: Rs.1000/-

Schedule
DATE : 11-04-2014
TIME : 00:00 to 00:00 hrs
VENUE : NB23
Judging Criteria
Judgment will be done on the basis of working/ principle/ design/ innovation and idea of the model. In case of any dispute judges decision shall be final.
