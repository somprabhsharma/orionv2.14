Googledia

Answer, Score and Win!!!
As Simple as That!!


Description
1. It is a quiz, in which participants can take part in a group of three. Two round will be conducted. Only four teams can participate at a time.
2. Teams participating at a time-4
3. No of members in a team-3
4. Maximum Time limit-1 Hour(20 min each round)
5. No of rounds-2+1(Optional Tie-breaker).
Rules and Regulations
1. Only team entries are eligible.
2. The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be subjected to any change.
3. The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronics instuments.
4. Audience shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors.
5. Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
6. Teams selected for the final rounds will be allowed to give themselves an appropriate name.
Gameplay
Round 1 - Buzzer round-
A question will be fired & the team who presses the buzzer first get to answer first- 6 Questions. There will be questionnaires on the computer fundamental knowledge.
1. 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong or if team doesn't after they press Buzzer.- No forwarding
2. Answering time is only 30 seconds
Round 2 - Image-visual round-
Participants have to guess the general errors in computer, by seeing the related images: 12 questions
1. 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong answer
2. Each team would be asked 2 audio-visual questions.(1 slide in each round)
3. Answering time is only 30 seconds.
4. No passing to the net team. Audience can answer.
Round 3 - Rapid Fire round-
Each team will be asked 10 questions one after another in one minute's time-: 60 questions
1. 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong answer
2. Answering time is only 30 seconds
3. Team discussion is allowed
4. If a team cannot answer the question, they can say pass for the next question. The question will not be forwarded to the next team.
Note
1. Be prepared to face no. of twists that will be introduced at any time.
2. Some gameplay criteria will be disclosed at venue.
